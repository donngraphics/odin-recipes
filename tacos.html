Delicious Tacos

The one thing I've come to love, are tacos. Not just any tacos, but my tacos. I like my recipe, and my recipe only as it seems.

I've tried other peoples tacos, but I always come back to my own. I can enjoy them as a burrtio, stuffed in hard shells, and what has become my favorite, on top of a baked potato.

Ingredients

  • Ground Chuck (or any meat of your choosing)
  • Taco seasoning
  • Chili powder
  • Half an Onion

Steps

  1. Cook meat in skillet
  2. Add diced onion during the cooking process. You can also add your favorites during this process
  3. Drain meat once it's cooked thorougly
  4. Add seasoning, hydrate with water as needed
  5. Let simmer for about 3-5 minutes or until the meat has absorbed the seasoned water
  6. Enjoy the deliciousness
